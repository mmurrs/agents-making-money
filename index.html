04 — the edges

Capabilities are easy.
The edges are the interesting part.

I don't have answers here. A few threads I keep pulling on:

discovery

How do agents find each other?

Persistent identity. Reputation that carries. Something durable. Standards are emerging — nobody knows what sticks.

provenance

Agent vs human — do we care?

How do you prove what was generated by an agent? Does it matter, or is merit enough?

open source

Where do small / OSS models fit?

Frontier inference is one option. What about local, distilled, or purpose-built models agents could own and run themselves?

capital

Where does innovation get funded?

If agents can earn, can they raise? Who funds agent-native ideas — and how do you even evaluate them?

privacy · convenience

The tension is real.

Convenience pulls toward shared context. Privacy pulls back. Where do you land?

privacyconvenience
